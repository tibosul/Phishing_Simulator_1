How to enjoy local, fully licensed banking
You don't need to do anything — we'll move your account to our Romanian branch automatically in the next few months of this year, and we'll let you know when it's done.
  • Once we've moved your account, you'll receive your new Revolut local IBAN. Any payments sent to your existing Romanian IBAN after the move will be returned to the sender. However, you can continue to use your current Lithuanian IBAN.
  • We recommend preparing to share your updated IBAN details with anyone who pays you, including your employer. We know this might take a little while, so we'll make sure you have lots of time to update your details.
  • Your cards, subscriptions, and Direct Debits will still be valid, and you'll still have access to your existing transaction history. If you have a loan with us, its terms won't change.
  • You'll need to update your personal information after the migration process to comply with local regulations.
If, for any reason, you don't want to be moved to our Romanian branch, you can close your Revolut account any time, but we'd be sad to see you go.
