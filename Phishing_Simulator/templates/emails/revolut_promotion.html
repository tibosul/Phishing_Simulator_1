How do I invite my friends?
  1. Open your app and tap the referral tile on the home screen
  2. Select the friends you want to refer from your contact list
You can get rewarded for up to 5 referred friends. They'll need to be eligible for a Revolut account and can't already be a Revolut customer.
What do my friends need to do?
To earn your reward, your friends must meet the conditions below within 14 days of signing up for a Revolut account using your referral link:
  1. Create a Revolut account using your unique referral link
  2. Order a physical Revolut card (delivery fees may apply)
  3. Spend a minimum of RON150 in-store
Purchases can be made with virtual or physical cards, and they can use Apple or Google Pay to start spending straight away.
Online payments aren't eligible towards the minimum spend. For more information, please see the full Referral T&Cs.
